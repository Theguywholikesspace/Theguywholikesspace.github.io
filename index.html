About Me

Hello there! I'm Adam Marcello, a freshman at Haverford College planning to major in Physics and Astronomy as well as a minor in Applied Math and Chemistry. My primary interest lies in exoplanets, particularly their formation and evolution from a stellar disk. I am fascinated by the processes that shape planetary systems and hope to contribute to our understanding of their origins, dynamics, and potential habitability. Through my studies, I aim to explore the physics governing these distant worlds and engage in research that advances our knowledge of planetary formation.
